Blog Entry 1: Inception of Radians

We were originally two groups which passed an inschool selection for young Enterprise, but to amass our prowess, we combine the teams for the potential to reach higher.

As we embarked on this unified path, our first focus was on building a strong foundation. Embracing the diversity within our collective, we recognized and respected the unique strengths of each member. This laid the groundwork for a resilient and united team, where individual skills seamlessly complemented one another.

The merger not only brought together different individuals but also injected fresh perspectives into our entrepreneurial endeavors. This collaborative innovation resulted in a more dynamic and creative environment. As we blended the best ideas and practices from both groups, one idea came on top - an approachable math game!

Blog Entry 2: Fundraising

All of the team have bought 20+ shares for the starting of the compnay. And with that intial funding, our further fundraising campaign started.

With the help of the whole team, we have decide and develope a game, which involves the player throwing a ping-pong ball, across a table, into three cans at the end. The player can at maximum bounce the ball twice on the table, and by getting it in, the player can win from a bags of candy to giant plushies. This game requires both hand eye coordinates, 3D trajectory caculation and analytical thinking to suceed.

We have also made christmas cards, with it's elegent design and dedicated hard work and the application of effective sales techniques, we managed to sell lots of them.

At the end of novermber, with the games we've held and christmas cards we have sold, we have generate an impressive profit of 800 pounds
